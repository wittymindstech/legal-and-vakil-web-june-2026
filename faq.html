Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Find quick answers about our services, booking process, and general legal compliance.

General Questions

What is Legal & Vakil?
Legal & Vakil is a premium digital legal platform connecting individuals and businesses with verified, top-tier lawyers, CAs, and corporate compliance experts in India. We offer transparent, flat-rate pricing and modern tools like our AI Legal Assistant.
How does the AI Legal Assistant work?
Our AI Legal Assistant (Beta) helps you understand basic legal concepts, rights, and regulatory procedures. It can analyze simple legal queries and point you toward the correct legal services, though it is not a replacement for formal advice from a qualified attorney.

Booking & Consultations

How do I book a consultation with a lawyer?
Browse our directory under "Find Lawyers", select an expert based on their specialty (e.g., criminal, family, or corporate law), and click "Consult Now" or "Book Call". Fill out the consultation request, and our system will schedule a secure phone or virtual session.
Are the consultation fees flat-rate?
Yes, we partner with verified legal professionals to offer flat-rate, transparent pricing. A standard consultation starts at a heavily discounted rate of ₹99 (originally ₹499) for a limited time.

Services & Drafting

What services are offered on the platform?
We offer extensive services across corporate setup (LLP/Pvt Ltd incorporation), Marriage Consultations, Property Disputes, Debt & Loan issues, Online Dispute Resolution (ODR), Intellectual Property filings (Trademark/Copyright/Patent), and Criminal Defense.
Can I get my business contracts drafted or reviewed?
Absolutely. Our corporate legal experts specialize in drafting and reviewing custom founder agreements, Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs), terms of service, employment contracts, and vendor agreements.

Payments & Refunds

Is my payment secure on Legal & Vakil?
Yes. All payments are processed through industry-standard encrypted payment gateways. Your billing details are never stored on our servers, ensuring 100% security.
Can I request a refund if my consultation doesn't happen?
Yes. If a scheduled consultation does not take place due to lawyer unavailability or platform technical issues, you are entitled to a full 100% refund. Please contact our support within 24 hours of the scheduled time.
